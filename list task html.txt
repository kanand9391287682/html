    <h3>Simple Pancakes</h3>
    <strong>Ingredients:</strong>
    <ul>
      <li>1 cup all-purpose flour</li>
      <li>1 cup milk</li>
      <li>1 egg</li>
      <li>1 tbsp sugar</li>
      <li>1 tsp baking powder</li>
      <li>Pinch of salt</li>
    </ul>

    <strong>Steps:</strong>
    <ol>
      <li>Mix dry ingredients in a bowl.</li>
      <li>Whisk milk and egg together, then combine with dry mix until smooth.</li>
      <li>Heat a non-stick pan, pour batter to form pancakes.</li>
      <li>Cook until bubbles appear, flip and cook the other side.</li>
      <li>Serve warm with syrup or fruit.</li>
    </ol>
